How Brands Calculate CPM

Brands evaluate influencer pricing through CPM (Cost Per Mille) lens:

CPM = (Sponsorship Rate √∑ Expected Views) √ó 1,000

Example:

  • Post rate: $1,000
  • Average views: 40,000
  • CPM: ($1,000 √∑ 40,000) √ó 1,000 = $25 CPM

Target CPM ranges by platform:

  • Instagram feed posts: $15-$30 CPM
  • Instagram Reels: $10-$25 CPM (higher reach)
  • Instagram Stories: $8-$20 CPM
  • Facebook ads (comparison): $10-$15 CPM

Brands accept higher CPM on Instagram because:

  • Better targeting (niche audiences)
  • Higher trust (creator endorsement)
  • Superior engagement rates
  • Integrated content (not obvious ads)

Nano vs Micro vs Macro Influencer Rates

How pricing and value differ by tier:

Nano Influencers (1K-10K)

Rate: $50-$250 per post
Strength: Highest engagement (6-8%), strong community trust
Best for: Local businesses, niche products, micro-budget campaigns
Negotiation: Often accept product-only deals, build portfolio

Micro Influencers (10K-50K)

Rate: $200-$1,000 per post
Strength: Best ROI for brands (4-6% engagement), authentic connections
Best for: E-commerce brands, DTC startups, performance campaigns
Negotiation: Mix of cash + product, performance bonuses common

Mid-Tier Influencers (50K-500K)

Rate: $1,000-$10,000 per post
Strength: Scale + quality balance, professional content
Best for: Established brands, product launches, awareness campaigns
Negotiation: Cash deals, exclusivity clauses, multi-post packages

Macro Influencers (500K-1M)

Rate: $10,000-$25,000 per post
Strength: Massive reach, celebrity status
Best for: Major brands, mass-market products, high-budget campaigns
Negotiation: Agent-managed, complex contracts, usage rights negotiations
