How to Set Your Rates

  • Cost-Based: Calculate your personal expenses + business costs + desired savings. Divide by billable hours.
  • Value-Based: Charge based on the value you provide to the client (e.g., if you make them $10k, charge $2k). Compare this to premium Instagram sponsorship rates.

Smart Tax Deductions for Freelancers

Lowering your taxable income is just as important as raising your rates. In 2026, most freelancers can deduct the following business expenses to increase their take-home pay:

  • Home Office Deduction: If you work from home, you can usually deduct a portion of your rent/mortgage and utilities.
  • Software & Subscriptions: Tools like Adobe Creative Cloud, Slack, and even your hosting fees for monetized blogs are deductible.
  • Marketing & Ads: Any money spent on LinkedIn Premium, portfolio sites, or even promoting your Instagram brand counts as a business expense.
  • Professional Development: Courses, books, and coaching to improve your skills.

Remember that the number shown in our Freelance Calculator is an estimate. Speak with a certified accountant to ensure you are maximizing your deductions and complying with your local tax laws.
