How TikTok Dropshipping Works

TikTok dropshipping combines viral content with e-commerce. You create TikTok videos showcasing products, drive traffic to your Shopify store (or TikTok Shop), and fulfill orders through suppliers like AliExpress, CJ Dropshipping, or AutoDS.

The revenue formula:

Step 1: Orders = Views √ó Conversion Rate

Step 2: Valid Orders = Orders √ó (1 - Refund Rate)

Step 3: Revenue = Valid Orders √ó Product Price

Step 4: Product Costs = Valid Orders √ó (Product Cost + Shipping)

Step 5: Net Profit = Revenue - Product Costs - Ad Spend

Break-Even ROAS Explained

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures revenue generated per dollar spent on advertising:

ROAS = Total Revenue √∑ Ad Spend

Break-even ROAS: The minimum ROAS needed to not lose money.

Break-Even ROAS = Selling Price √∑ (Product Cost + Shipping Cost)

Example:

  • Product price: $35
  • Product cost: $12
  • Shipping: $3
  • Break-even ROAS: $35 √∑ $15 = 2.33√ó

This means you need $2.33 in revenue for every $1 in ad spend to break even. Target 2.5-3.0√ó for healthy profitability.
