How to Calculate Instagram Growth Rate

Instagram growth rate measures follower increase as a percentage over time:

Growth Rate = ((Current Followers - Previous Followers) √∑ Previous Followers) √ó 100

Example calculation:

  • Previous followers: 40,000
  • Current followers: 45,000
  • Net gain: 5,000
  • Growth rate: (5,000 √∑ 40,000) √ó 100 = 12.5%

Normalizing to Monthly Rate

If measured over different period, normalize to monthly:

Monthly Growth = (Total Growth √∑ Days) √ó 30

Example: 12.5% over 45 days
‚Üí (12.5% √∑ 45) √ó 30 = 8.3% monthly

How Brands Evaluate Influencer Growth

Brands analyze growth through multiple lenses:

1. Growth Consistency

Brands prefer steady growth over erratic spikes:

  • Ideal: 3-5% monthly for 6+ consecutive months
  • Red flag: 20% one month, -5% next month (suggests bot buying)
  • Audit method: Brands check growth charts in Social Blade, HypeAuditor

2. Growth-to-Engagement Ratio

Growth must correlate with engagement:

Scenario Assessment Brand Action
High growth + High engagement Ideal Premium rates, long-term partnerships
High growth + Low engagement Suspicious Audit for fake followers, lower rates
Slow growth + High engagement Quality Niche partnerships, micro-influencer value
Slow growth + Low engagement Stagnant Pass or low-budget only

4. Brand Attractiveness Score

Combination of growth + engagement creates brand value:

High Brand Attractiveness

  • Growth rate: 3-8% monthly

  • Follower authenticity: 85%+ real
  • Content quality: Professional + consistent

Monthly vs Annual Growth Explained

Understanding compound growth mathematics:

Monthly Growth (Simple)

Monthly Growth % = (Net Gain √∑ Starting Followers) √ó 100

Annualized Growth (Compounded)

Annual Growth = (1 + Monthly Rate)^12 - 1

Example: 3% monthly growth

  • Month 1: 40,000 ‚Üí 41,200 (+3%)
  • Month 2: 41,200 ‚Üí 42,436 (+3%)
  • Month 3: 42,436 ‚Üí 43,709 (+3%)
  • Continue for 12 months...
  • Year-end: 57,037 followers
  • Annual growth: 42.6% (compounded from 3% monthly)
