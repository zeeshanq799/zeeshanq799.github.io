How Much Should You Charge for Instagram Stories?

Instagram story pricing follows different economics than feed posts due to their ephemeral nature:

Pricing Method Formula Best For
Feed Post % 30-50% of feed post rate Standard sponsorships, brand awareness
Reach-Based CPM (Story Views √∑ 1,000) √ó $10-$20 High-reach accounts, Reels crossposting
Performance-Based Base fee + % of sales Affiliate links, direct response

Why Stories Cost Less Than Feed Posts

  • 24-hour lifespan: No long-term portfolio value
  • Lower engagement: 20-40% less than feed posts typically
  • Less production time: Authentic, casual format expected
  • No permanent visibility: Unless saved to Highlights (charge premium)

When Stories Command Premium Pricing

  • Swipe-up conversions: Proven track record of sales
  • Highlight placement: 30-90 day visibility = 30-50% premium
  • Time-sensitive campaigns: Product launches, flash sales
  • Exclusive access: Behind-the-scenes, event coverage
