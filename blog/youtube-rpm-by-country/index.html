Which Country Has the Highest YouTube RPM in 2026?

The United States has the highest average YouTube RPM in 2026, typically ranging between $8 and $20+ depending on niche. Finance and SaaS channels targeting American audiences can exceed $25 RPM. Other high-paying countries include the UK, Canada, Australia, and Germany.

These RPM values represent average creator-reported data. Actual earnings may vary depending on audience retention, ad types shown, advertiser seasonality, and niche competition.

Tier 1 vs Tier 2 vs Tier 3 Countries Explained

RPM varies significantly depending on niche, audience engagement, watch time, and advertiser demand. Finance and SaaS channels targeting Tier 1 countries like the United States or UK can earn 3x‚Äì10x more compared to entertainment channels targeting developing markets.

Tier 1 Countries (Highest RPM)

Tier 1 countries include the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. These regions consistently produce the highest YouTube RPM because advertisers compete aggressively for audiences with strong purchasing power.

Creators targeting Tier 1 traffic often earn 3x‚Äì10x more compared to creators targeting developing markets, even with the same number of views.

Tier 2 Countries (Moderate RPM)

Tier 2 countries include Germany, France, UAE, Spain, and parts of Europe. RPM is typically strong but slightly lower than Tier 1 markets due to lower ad competition or smaller advertiser budgets.

Tier 3 Countries (Lower RPM)

Tier 3 countries include India, Pakistan, Philippines, Indonesia, Bangladesh, and many emerging markets. While these regions generate high view volume, advertiser budgets are lower, resulting in lower RPM.

However, creators can still generate strong revenue in Tier 3 markets by increasing watch time, targeting high-value niches like finance or SaaS, and attracting some percentage of international viewers.

Why Does RPM Differ by Country?

1. Advertiser Competition

Countries with more advertisers competing for audiences typically generate higher CPM and RPM.

2. Purchasing Power

Higher-income countries attract higher ad budgets because viewers are more likely to convert into paying customers.

3. Niche Demand

Finance and SaaS content targeting USA traffic can generate significantly higher RPM compared to entertainment content targeting developing markets.

How to Target High-RPM Countries on YouTube

If your current audience is primarily from lower-RPM regions, you can strategically optimize your channel to attract higher-value traffic.

Finance, investing, AI tools, business software, and SaaS tutorials consistently attract higher-paying advertisers.

3. Improve Watch Time

Longer watch sessions allow YouTube to serve more ads, increasing total RPM. Aim for 8+ minute videos when possible.
