TikTok Creativity Program RPM Per 1000 Views in 2026

Last Updated: February 17, 2026 · 12 min read

The TikTok Creativity Program has significantly increased creator payouts compared to the old Creator Fund. In 2026, the TikTok Creativity Program RPM per 1000 views ranges between $0.40 and $1.20 depending on audience location, engagement quality, and niche.

How Much Does TikTok Creativity Program Pay Per 1000 Views?

Under the Creativity Program, TikTok pays creators for qualified views. Longer videos (1 minute or more) with strong retention generate higher payouts.

Country Estimated RPM
United States $0.80 – $1.20
United Kingdom $0.70 – $1.00
Canada $0.60 – $0.90
Australia $0.60 – $0.95
India $0.20 – $0.50

What Affects TikTok Creativity Program RPM?

  • Audience country (Tier 1 countries generate higher advertiser demand)
  • Watch time and completion rate
  • Video length (minimum 1 minute required)
  • Ad demand and seasonality
  • Content niche (finance, business, and tech perform better)

Real Earnings Example

If a creator receives 500,000 qualified views in a month at an average RPM of $0.90, their estimated earnings would be:

500,000 ÷ 1,000 × $0.90 = $450

Creators targeting Tier 1 countries like the United States or United Kingdom can often achieve RPM above $1.00 during high advertiser seasons.

How to Increase Your TikTok RPM

  • Create longer storytelling content (1–3 minutes)
  • Target US, UK, Canada, and Australia audiences
  • Focus on finance, tech, and business topics
  • Improve retention in first 5 seconds
  • Post consistently to improve account authority

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does TikTok Creativity Program pay per 1000 views?

In 2026, RPM typically ranges from $0.40 to $1.20 depending on location and engagement quality.

What is TikTok RPM?

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) represents the amount a creator earns per 1000 monetized views after TikTok's share.

Why is my TikTok RPM low?

Low RPM can result from short watch time, audience in lower-paying regions, or reduced advertiser demand.

The TikTok Creativity Program in 2026 offers significantly higher payouts than the old Creator Fund. However, RPM depends heavily on audience geography and engagement quality.
